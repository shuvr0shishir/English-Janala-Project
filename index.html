Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and vocabulary sections. These resources are designed to help you gradually improve your English skills step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes, this website is completely free to use. You can access lessons, vocabulary, and practice materials without any cost.
3. Do I need to create an account?
No, you can explore most of the lessons and vocabulary without creating an account. However, creating an account may allow you to track your progress and access additional features.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can build your vocabulary by practicing regularly, learning new words from lessons, and using interactive exercises available on the website.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Currently, this website focuses on learning and practice. Certificates may be offered in the future after completing certain courses or lessons.
